                                      Preparation

                                      FOR BEST QUALITY, THAW UNOPENED BAG FOR 15-24 HOURS IN THE REFRIGERATOR (40°F) IN A SINGLE LAYER. ONCE THAWED, OPEN REFRIGERATED GUACAMOLE WITHIN 7 DAYS.OPENED BAG: KEEP SURFACE COVERED TO PREVENT BROWNING AND STORE AT 40°F.ONCE THAWED, DO NOT REFREEZE.
