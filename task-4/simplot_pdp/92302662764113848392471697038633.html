                                      Preparation

                                      Preparation

                                      FOR FOOD SAFETY AND QUALITY, FOLLOW THESE COOKING INSTRUCTIONS TO ENSURE PRODUCT REACHES AN INTERNAL TEMPERATURE OF 165°F.

                                      Convection Oven
                                      CONVECTION OVEN *Bake potatoes at 350°F for 15-20 minutes in a single layer on a greased sheet pan. Rotate halfway through cook time.
                                      Microwave
                                      MICROWAVE (1100 WATTS) *Microwave ½ bag of potatoes on HIGH for 7 minutes, covered, stirring halfway through cooking time. Let stand for 1 minute.* If desired, add 1-2 Tbsp. butter before cooking.
